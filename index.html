Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Barrel, a property management app designed to help you organize and track your rental properties. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection and Storage

Barrel is designed with your privacy in mind:

  • Local Storage Only: All your property data, tenant information, payment records, and documents are stored exclusively on your device using iCloud (if enabled).
  • No Server Uploads: We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any analytics services that track your usage or behavior.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Since we don't collect your data, we have nothing to share with third parties.

Subscription and Payment Information

Barrel offers a yearly subscription with a 7-day free trial, managed entirely through Apple's In-App Purchase system:

  • Subscription: $19.99 per year after the 7-day free trial
  • Payment Processing: All payment transactions are handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.
  • Subscription Data: Apple provides us with minimal information to verify your subscription status (active, trial, or expired), but we do not receive your personal or payment details.
  • Trial Tracking: We use Apple's subscription status to determine trial eligibility and expiration.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync on your device:

  • Your Barrel data is synchronized across your Apple devices using your personal iCloud account.
  • This data is encrypted and managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Face ID / Touch ID

If you enable biometric authentication:

  • Face ID and Touch ID authentication is handled entirely by iOS.
  • We do not store or have access to your biometric data.
  • This feature is optional and can be disabled in Settings.

Photos and Documents

When you add photos or documents to your properties:

  • These files are stored locally on your device or in your iCloud storage.
  • We request permission to access your photo library only when you choose to add photos.
  • We do not access, upload, or share these files.

Notifications

If you enable notifications:

  • We send local notifications to remind you of rent due dates and other important events.
  • These notifications are generated locally on your device.
  • We do not send your data to push notification services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
