Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Barrel ("the App") is a rental property management app developed for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Barrel — including properties, units, tenants, contracts, and payments — is stored locally on your device only. Your data may be backed up through your device's iCloud backup (if enabled in iOS Settings), but no data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party by the App itself.

Data Collection

Barrel does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used.
  • No account creation or login is required.
  • No data is sent over the internet by the App.
  • No cookies or identifiers are used.

Subscription and Payment Information

Barrel offers an auto-renewable annual subscription managed entirely through Apple's In-App Purchase system:

  • Payment Processing: All payment transactions are handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.
  • Subscription Data: Apple provides us with minimal information to verify your subscription status (active, trial, or expired), but we do not receive your personal or payment details.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data processing services. The only external interaction is with Apple's App Store for in-app purchases, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

Barrel may request permission to send local notifications for payment reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external server.

Face ID / Touch ID

If you enable biometric authentication:

  • Face ID and Touch ID authentication is handled entirely by iOS.
  • We do not store or have access to your biometric data.
  • This feature is optional and can be disabled in Settings.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete all app data at any time by deleting the App from your device. There is no remote data to request deletion of.

Children's Privacy

Barrel is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
